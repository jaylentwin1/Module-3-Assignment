About Us

We have been in the community for over ten years! Our goal has always been to give back to the community whether that be by providing great customer service or by hosting many fun activities and programs in order to promote togetherness in our community. We are so much more than a Coffeeehouse that primarily sells great cups of coffee. We are a key figure in our city.
